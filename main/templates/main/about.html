About

A simple project, to fight media-censorship.

Hi, my name is Dyotson and I'm a little software developer from Chile. I saw what was happening is social media all over the Israel and Palestine conflict and decided to do something about it.

What this web does is get the last updated posts every 10 minutes from popular front instagram page, and saves them locally. So, if instagram deletes a post, you can see it here.

The project is Open Source with GNU GPLv3 license and you can find it at GitHub.

This page is not monetized in any way or form and I don't plan to do so, unless the creator of the page @popular.front contacts me to do something about this project.
